# Customized Contra Layout

 This is for an mit layout Contra.


## To create a hex:

`make contra:sarahjean_mit`


## To reset board for flashing:

Hold raise + lower, and press q


## QWERTY (Normal) Layer
```
 ,-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------.
 | Tab  |   Q  |   W  |   E  |   R  |   T  |   Y  |   U  |   I  |   O  |   P  | Bksp |
 |------+------+------+------+------+-------------+------+------+------+------+------|
 | ESC  |   A  |   S  |   D  |   F  |   G  |   H  |   J  |   K  |   L  |   ;  |  '   |
 |------+------+------+------+------+------|------+------+------+------+------+------|
 | Shift|   Z  |   X  |   C  |   V  |   B  |   N  |   M  |   ,  |   .  |   /  |Enter |
 |------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------|
 |      | Ctrl | Alt  | GUI  |Lower | Space| Space|Raise | Left |Right |  Up  |Down  |
 `-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------'
```
